Install Connect HR on your phone

Connect HR can be installed directly from your browser. No app-store download is required.

Android - Google Chrome
1. Open the Connect HR link in Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
3. Select Install app or Add to Home screen.
4. Tap Install, then open Connect HR from your home screen.
iPhone - Safari
1. Open the Connect HR link in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Select Add to Home Screen.
4. Tap Add, then open Connect HR from your home screen.
